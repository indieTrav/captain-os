# Customer Success Stories

_Case studies and wins. Source material for content, sales pages, and social proof._

---

## How to Build a Success Story

1. Identify a customer with a strong before/after
2. Get on a 15-minute call or ask for written answers
3. Questions: What was your situation before? What did you try? What changed? What specific result did you get?
4. Write it up using the template below
5. Get approval before publishing

---

## Story Template

**Name:** [First name + last initial, or full name if they agreed]
**Context:** [Role, industry, business stage]
**Product:** [Which product they bought]

**The Before:**
[2-3 sentences on their situation before — use their words if possible]

**The Struggle:**
[What they had tried, what wasn't working]

**The After:**
[The specific result — with numbers when possible]

**In Their Words:**

> "[Direct quote — this is what goes on the sales page]"
