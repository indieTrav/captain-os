# Roadmap

Big-picture projects and initiatives, sequenced by horizon. One scrollable view of where the business is heading.

- **Now** = active or starting in the next 4–6 weeks. Each item should map to a brief in `projects/`.
- **Next** = on deck, queued behind Now. Validated direction, not yet started.
- **Later** = directional bets, parking lot, big ideas worth keeping warm. May or may not happen.

Move items down the list as horizons shift. Archive completed ones to `99-archives/` with a one-line outcome note.

## Notes

- Roadmap ≠ goals. `goals/` = outcomes by date (revenue, customers). Roadmap = the _initiatives_ that produce those outcomes.
- Roadmap ≠ projects. `projects/` = the active execution brief for one initiative. Roadmap = the sequence of initiatives across time.
- Roadmap ≠ ideas. `ideas.md` = raw capture. Roadmap = ideas that have graduated to "this is happening, on this horizon."
