# Decision Log

*Major decisions and the reasoning behind them. Append-only. Newest at top.*

---

## Why this exists

Future-you (and future hires) will ask "why did we do that?" This is the answer file. Write entries when:
- A decision affects more than one team or department
- A decision is hard to reverse
- A decision overrides a previous decision

Use `99-library/templates/os_templates/decision_log_entry_template.md` for each entry.
