# DM / Outreach Script

*For initiating conversations with potential buyers on LinkedIn, Instagram, or other platforms.*

---

## Outreach Principles

1. Lead with value or genuine curiosity — never pitch cold
2. Reference something specific about them
3. One ask per message (don't double-ask)
4. Follow-up once, then leave it

---

## Opening Messages

### Version A — Content Engagement Follow-Up

> _[Reference their comment/post on [topic], show you understood it, then ask one curious question about the problem they may have.]_

---

### Version B — Mutual Connection / Community

> _[Reference the shared community/group, note what they post about, then ask about a common challenge.]_

---

### Version C — Cold but Specific

> _[Reference how you found them, name who you work with and the outcome, cite a specific detail, then ask about the problem they likely have.]_

---

## Follow-Up (If No Reply in 5–7 Days)

> _[Follow up once, offer a piece of value (link or tip), keep it low-pressure.]_

---

## Transition to Offer (Once Rapport Is Established)

> _[Name the problem they described, introduce your offer for that audience, ask if you can share details.]_

---

## What NOT to Do

- Don't pitch in the first message
- Don't use templates verbatim — customize every message
- Don't follow up more than twice
- Don't send voice notes as first contact
