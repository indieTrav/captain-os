# Discovery Call Script

*For 30-minute calls with potential buyers. Goal: understand their situation → see if you can help → make an offer if appropriate.*

---

## Call Structure (30 min)

- **0–2 min:** Small talk + set the agenda
- **2–10 min:** Understand their situation (ask, listen, don't pitch)
- **10–20 min:** Dig into the problem and what it's costing them
- **20–25 min:** Present your offer (only if it's a fit)
- **25–30 min:** Handle objections + close or next step
