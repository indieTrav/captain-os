# Trends & Bets

_Market signals I'm seeing now, and the bets I'm making on where they go next. Every bet should be grounded in a trend below it — if a bet has no observable signal, it's a guess._

---

## How to use this file

- **Current Trends** = what's happening in the market right now that I can point to (data, posts, launches, conversations, behavior shifts).
- **Future Bets** = what I think happens next, and how I'm positioning for it. Each bet links back to one or more current trends.
- Review monthly. Kill trends that turned out to be noise. Promote bets that paid off into positioning/offers. Demote bets that didn't.
