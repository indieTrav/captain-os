# Offer Types — Menu

A picker for offer-variation generation. Each item is a distinct *offer shape*. When generating variations, span the menu — don't cluster three flavors of "discount".

## Pricing & Payment
- **Discount** — %, $, or BOGO. Cheap signal, trains negotiability.
- **Tiered Pricing** — good / better / best at distinct price points.
- **Intro Pricing** — lower price on first month or first purchase.
- **Pre-Order / Pre-Sale** — pre-release price for committing early.
- **Founding Member** — one-time discounted price for first cohort, often with lifetime perks.
- **Lifetime Deal** — pay once, access forever; validates demand and front-loads cash.
- **Payment Plan / BNPL** — split price into installments.
- **Zero-Interest Financing** — formal financing for larger tickets.
- **Leasing** — long-term flexible payment, B2B / capital goods.
- **Name-Your-Price** — buyer chooses within bounds.
- **Auto-Renew Discount** — discount for opting into auto-renew.
- **Progressive / Bulk Discount** — more spend = bigger discount.
- **Multibuy** — 3-for-2 type savings.

## Trial & Access
- **Free Trial** — time-bound free access.
- **Paid Trial** — small fee for trial period; filters serious buyers.
- **Free Consultation** — service-biz lead magnet.
- **Home Trial** — try at home pre-purchase (physical).
- **Product Sample** — small free taste.
- **Demo / Walkthrough** — guided live experience.

## Bundles & Add-ons
- **Bundle** — multiple items at lower total than separate.
- **Slow-Mover Bundle** — pair hit product with slow-moving stock.
- **Cross-Sell** — discounted related add-on at checkout.
- **Upsell** — upgrade to higher tier at discount.
- **Gift with Purchase** — bonus item included.
- **Free Personalization** — customize the product included.
- **Free Installation / Setup** — remove implementation friction.
- **Free Maintenance / Support** — ongoing post-purchase service.
- **Extended Warranty** — longer coverage as added value.
- **Free Storage / Cloud Space** — capacity add-on (tech).

## Guarantees & Risk Reversal
- **Unconditional Guarantee** — full refund, no questions.
- **Conditional Guarantee** — refund only if buyer did X, Y, Z.
- **Anti-Guarantee** — all sales final + credible reason (damaging admission).
- **Performance / Revshare** — paid only when buyer wins.
- **Price-Match Guarantee** — match lower price elsewhere.
- **Price-Drop Refund** — refund difference if price drops after purchase.
- **Free Returns** — remove return-risk friction.

## Subscriptions & Membership
- **Subscription Pricing** — recurring at discount vs. one-time.
- **Free Extra Month** — bonus time on annual signup.
- **Membership Extension** — add term to existing sub.
- **Exclusive Member Sale** — members-only event/pricing.
- **Tiered Loyalty Program** — escalating benefits with usage.
- **Product-of-the-Month Club** — recurring curated shipment.

## Loyalty & Referral
- **Loyalty Points / Rewards** — repeat-purchase points.
- **Referral Program** — reward customers who bring others.
- **Refer-a-Friend, Get a Month Free** — referral for subscriptions.
- **Customer Anniversary Discount** — reward time-as-customer.
- **Birthday Offer** — personal-date discount or gift.
- **Reactivation Offer** — special deal to inactive customers.
- **Next-Purchase Discount** — post-purchase credit.
- **Cart-Abandonment Offer** — recovery email with incentive.

## Scarcity & Urgency
- **Flash Sale** — short-window discount.
- **Limited Edition** — capped supply of distinct version.
- **Last Chance / End-of-Line** — discontinuing items.
- **Back-in-Stock** — restocked popular item.
- **Exit Offer** — pop-up before leaving site.
- **Early Access / Early Bird** — pre-launch privilege.

## Cohort, Cap, Container
- **Cohort** — fixed-date enrollment, group moves together.
- **Total Business Cap** — only ever serve X clients.
- **Growth Rate Cap** — only X new clients per week / month.
- **Cohort Cap** — X seats per cohort, fixed cohorts per year.

## Audience / Segment-Specific
- **Group-Specific Discount** — students, seniors, military, teachers, healthcare, first responders, family/friends.
- **First-Order Discount** — incentivize first-time buyers.
- **Personalized Offer** — based on individual behavior / data.

## Events, Seasons, Causes
- **Holiday / Seasonal Deal** — Black Friday, holiday season, etc.
- **Event-Based Promotion** — local, cultural, sports tie-in.
- **Product Launch Offer** — release-tied promo.
- **Product Launch Invitation** — exclusive invite to launch.
- **Charitable Donation Tie-in** — % of sales to cause.
- **Eco-Friendly Offer** — incentivize sustainable choice.
- **Carbon Offset** — built into each purchase.
- **Non-Profit Partnership** — sustained co-marketing with cause.

## Partnerships
- **Collaborative Promotion** — partner with non-competitor in niche.
- **Influencer Collaboration** — co-branded discount or gift.
- **Affiliate Discount** — affiliate-only unique code.
- **Sponsorship / Co-Marketing** — sponsor event for cross-promo.

## Content / Lead-Gen Offers
- **Digital Download** — free ebook, PDF, infographic.
- **Free Digital Product** — templates, design assets, swipe files.
- **Training / Webinar / Workshop** — live or recorded teaching.
- **Email Subscription Offer** — discount/freebie for signup.
- **Survey Completion Deal** — discount for filling survey.

## Engagement / UGC
- **Competition / Giveaway** — buzz play.
- **User-Generated Content Contest** — customer entries.
- **Customer Photo Competition** — product-in-use UGC.
- **Retweet / Share Contest** — viral reach.
- **Product-Based Challenge** — engagement around the product.
- **Social Media Check-in Deal** — discount for tagging.
- **Exclusive Social Media Offer** — channel-specific promo.

## Experiential & Premium
- **Experiential Reward** — exclusive event / tour / talk instead of discount.
- **Virtual Event / Experience** — online-exclusive happening.
- **Personal Shopping Experience** — high-tier white-glove service.
- **Limited-Time Upgrade** — higher tier at lower-tier price.
- **Free Upgrade** — comp upgrade for window.

## Cash, Credit, Logistics
- **Free Shipping** — cost-removal (e-commerce).
- **Cash Back** — % returned after purchase.
- **Trade-In** — old product for credit on new.
- **Recycling Discount** — return packaging/product for discount.
- **Credit for Future Purchases** — store credit unlock at threshold.
- **Gift Card with Purchase** — free gift card above spend tier.
- **Virtual Coupon** — trackable digital code.
- **Price Drop Alert** — proactive email when watched item drops.

## Misc / Surprise
- **Mystery Discount** — unknown amount revealed at checkout.
- **Freebie / Swag** — non-product item (stickers, pins).
- **Free Gift Wrapping** — seasonal value-add.
- **Employee Pick** — featured curated product with discount.
