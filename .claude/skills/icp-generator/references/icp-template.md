## Notes on filling the template

- **Italicized intro line** under each section header (e.g., "*Every piece of content...*") — keep this exact phrasing from the user's reference example, it sets the tone of the doc.
- **Blockquote format** (`> "..."`) for verbatim quotes — visually separates language from analysis.
- **Tables** for the pains/desires/sources sections — they scan fast and force concision.
- **Em dashes** (`—`) over hyphens when separating clauses in prose. Match the founders-vault voice.
- **Bold the labels** in the labeled-prose sections ("**Biggest frustration:**", etc.) — not headings, just bold lead-ins.
